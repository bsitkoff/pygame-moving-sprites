## Playtest and Reflect

Find someone else at roughly the same pace and play each other's games for 2 minutes.

**Give feedback on:**
- Is the player speed comfortable — not too fast, not too slow?
- Does the player stay on screen in a way that feels right?
- What would make this game more interesting?
